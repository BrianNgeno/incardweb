About InCAD

Established in 2011, InCAD is a professional training institution dedicated to promoting organizational performance by researching and empowering staff through practical and demand-driven programs.

We offer a wide range of learning solutions designed to meet the specific training needs of employees at different organizational levels. Through research, capacity building, and ethical professional practice, we help organizations improve competence, performance, and productivity.

What We Do

At InCAD, we specialize in capacity development solutions that enhance human performance, strengthen institutional effectiveness, and support professional growth. Our services are designed for:
